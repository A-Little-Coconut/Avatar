Meet the Characters

Iroh

Iroh is a retired Fire Nation General, a Grand Lotus of the Order of the White Lotus, a former Crown Prince, a firebending master, and the uncle of Zuko. Iroh mentors and watches over Zuko. As a firebending master, Iroh has earned his repuation by his ability to breathe fire. This earned him the title "The Dragon of the West". Iroh and his nephew, Zuko, travel all over the world to find Avatar Aang and put an end to him so that the fire nation can contune to rule over the nations.
