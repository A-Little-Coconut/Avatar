Meet the Characters

Sokka

Sokka was a warrior from the Southern Water Tribe and the sun of Chief Hakoda and Kya. Sokka and his sister Katara were raised by their grandmother after their mother died and their father went to go fight in the war. When Sokka and Katara discovered Avatar Aang frozen in an iceberg he was initially skeptical, but decided to help Aang on his quest to save the world.
